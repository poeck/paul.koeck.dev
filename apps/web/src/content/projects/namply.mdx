Coming up with a good brand name is one of those tasks that seems simple until you actually sit down to do it. You need something memorable, available as a domain, and not already taken by someone else. Most naming tools charge per session or require a subscription, which felt unnecessary for something that should be quick and painless.

I built Namply as a free alternative. You describe your project in a few words, and it generates dozens of creative name suggestions with real-time domain availability checking across popular TLDs. No signup, no credit card, no limits.

Everything runs locally where possible. Saved names and lists stay in your browser's localStorage, so there is nothing to sync or manage. The tool is designed to get out of your way and let you find a name fast.
